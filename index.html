Sun blanched white seashore. Endless beaches. Incessant blue waves, mostly calm, but sometimes boisterous, clamouring and vanishing among white splashes. Green groves of coconuts just stop shore and border the beaches with frills. Lurking in the backdrop a network of lagoons and backwaters. Boats and country crafts moving along. Quite a lot of rivers and countless rivulets flowing through hills and paddy fields of the midland, emptying themselves into the backwaters.And the far off dark blue mountains. This is what the eyes meet about Kerala. An excellent colour scheme and art work of Nature. An occasional boatsman rowing and singing with cargo or people. A tiller or a mower and women planting seedlings. Women at work spinning coir. Dense plantations further eastward. Kerala is all these and more.

Better known as 'Gods Own Country', Kerala offers you a host of exciting holiday options. Spread across the year are specially designed packages that highlight the State's attractions, and prove beyond doubt that the season never ends in this breath takingly beautiful land.
India's most idyllic State, Kerala is today one of the most sought after tourist destinations in the world. Today Kerala Tourism is a global super brand and is recognized as a pioneer and trend setter in the country. Its unique culture and traditions, coupled with its varied demography has made Kerala one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. Apart from being a tourist destination, Kerala is also India's most advanced society, cleanest and most peaceful state.

Responsible Tourism' has been adopted as the cornerstone for tourism development in the State
